Introduction

My name is Chantise Johnson. I was born on Febraury 5th in Akron, Ohio. I graduated from North High School in 2014. Some activities I enjoy include: cooking, traveling, and spending time with my family. The purpose of this site is to present information about my issues with self-apprehension, and to help others break the barriers of procrastination and self-doubt. My mission is to encourage others to not be afraid to take on new endeavors and push through their most challenging moments.

About Me

For my undergraduate education, I attended the University of Akron. I originally planned to major in biomedical engineering, but I was too focused on the challenges and became discouraged. I enjoy helping people, so I decided to pursue social work instead. Upon graduating with my BSW in 2018, I went on to grad school at Cleveland State University. There, I completed the advanced standing program and received my MSW in 2019. I ultimately decided that social work was not the field for me, so I have been employed at Amazon (AKC1) since 2020.

Software Development Journey

I have been yearning for more out of life and I do not want to work at Amazon forever, so I researched their Career Choice program. I have always had a general interest in computers, but I was not aware of the available career paths associated with them, or if I was even capable of achieving success in such a field. I decided to "just go for it" this time and HERE I AM!

The journey to becoming a software developer has been challenging, yet rewarding. I enjoy having the freedom to be innovative and use problem-solving skills. Thus far, I am hopeful about my future in the field as long as I remain consistent and dedicated. So far, I have learned that there are a plethora of key components of webpages and attention to detail is incredibly important to become a successful web developer. The most interesting parts of software development are that the learning process never ceases and that creative ability is endless. Anything can be accomplished with the correct application of knowledge.

Explore the Site

In the navigation bar above, you will have several options to explore on how you to can break the continuous cycle of procrastination and self-apprehension. Each tab has a different form of content that will aid you in beginning your journey towards increasing self motivation and self-esteem. In the Resources tab, expect to find links to worksheets, exercises, videos and activities. The Therapy tab will link you to websites where you can set up sessions with licensed therapists who specialize in Motivational Enhancement Therapy (MET). The Affirmations tab will lead you to positive self-affirmations that you can repeat to yourself anytime you are in need of encouragement. Finally, the Testimonials tab presents real-life stories of individuals just like us who have learned to overcome self-doubt. Please refer to the Contact section at the bottom of the page to sign up for news letters regarding my progress with the program and life in general.
